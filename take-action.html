TAKE ACTION

Making small changes when choosing what to buy and eat can contribute to significant environmental benefits. Below are steps you can take to help!

Check out a map of organic farms near you! This one shows farms local to Cary, NC.

Action Items

  • Include more plants in your diet. Cut down on animal products and eat more vegetables, fruits, and grains.
  • Eat fresh foods - plastic packaging and processing increases energy use.
  • Buy foods that have sustainability certifications or are labelled "organic" in order to avoid synthetic pesticides and fertilizers.
  • Buy only what you need and save leftovers.
  • Understand expiration dates and food date labels!
